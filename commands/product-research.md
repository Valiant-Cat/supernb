# product-research

```text
Use supernb command: product-research
Goal: <produce approved research evidence for the current initiative>
Context:
- repository: <optional url or local path>
- product category: <category or problem space>
- seed competitors: <known apps or products if known>
- markets: <target countries or regions>
- research window: <date range or recency window>
- initiative id: <existing initiative id if known>
- constraints: <scope, platform, monetization, or compliance constraints>
Output:
- collect a rich enough evidence set to support product decisions, not a shallow memo
- compare competitor feature surfaces, monetization, and regional positioning
- synthesize global user complaints, delights, feature requests, and jobs-to-be-done from reviews
- establish category headroom, repeatability, and growth signals strong enough for a 10M-DAU-class ambition
- save competitor landscape, review insights, and feature opportunities artifacts
- prepare the research artifact set for certification
```
